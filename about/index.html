About

Origin is FreeAgent’s internal CSS framework.

What and why

We’re a growing team working across a range of different projects and codebases. Making CSS work at scale is hard. Agreed principles, approaches, and patterns mean we can do better work faster.

Origin gives us a common way of writing CSS. It consumes our CSS utilities, provides some plug-and-play components, and also documents our principles and HTML and CSS code styles. We use it in our desktop app, mobile apps, website, and a bunch of other projects.
