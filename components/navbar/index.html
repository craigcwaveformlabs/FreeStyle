NavBar

The NavBar is the section in each of our apps which contains the main view heading along with the primary (and often the secondary) navigation.

Usage Note: Because of the difference between the nav bars in each of our projects, the basic component is rarely used on its own. It should be combined with one of the variants below.

Variants

  • Variants
  • Desktop

Desktop

The desktop app variant adds desktop-specific padding to the top of the NavBar and margins to the left and bottom of the title.

NavBar Title 

<nav class="NavBar NavBar--desktop" role="navigation">
  <h1 class="NavBar-title">
    <a class="NavBar-title-link" href="#"> <!-- optional -->
      NavBar Title
    </a>
  </h1>
</nav>
